# References

The third-party source papers replicated and critiqued in this project are **not
redistributed here** (they are copyrighted by their respective journals/publishers).
Please consult the originals via the citations below. This project's own replication
write-ups (`*_replication.tex` / `.pdf`) and findings (`findings.md`) remain in their
respective subdirectories.

## Momentum / previous-game effects (`clay_momentum_analysis/`)

- Clay, D.C. & Bro, A.S. (2015). "Previous game success as a determinant of future
  game performance and outcomes in men's NCAA basketball." *International Journal of
  Sport Psychology*, 46: 441–455.

## Travel & timezone effects (`clay_travel_analysis/`)

- Clay, D.C., Bro, A.S., & Clay, N.J. (2015). "Geospatial Determinants of Game
  Outcomes in NCAA Men's Basketball." *International Journal of Sport and Society*,
  Vol. 4.
- Clay, D.C., Bro, A.S., & Clay, N.J. (2014). "How Does Travel Affect NCAA Basketball
  Outcomes?" *Winthrop Intelligence*, Feb 17, 2014. (Popular-audience summary of the
  IJSS article above.)
